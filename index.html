Darwen Masters Swimming Club

Get in touch

Established in 1988, Darwen Masters has been providing high quality structured swimming opportunities for adults from Darwen and the surrounding areas for over 30 years.

The club caters for swimmers of all abilities - so whether swimming is important to you for health and wellbeing or you want to enter masters swimming galas - Darwen Masters is the place for you

We also have members who are keen on open water swimming and members who want to improve their swimming as part of their triathlon training
